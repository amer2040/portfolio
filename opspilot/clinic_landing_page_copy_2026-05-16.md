## Safety Section

### Section Headline
Designed to support clinic teams, not replace clinical judgment.

The assistant must not:
- Diagnose patient conditions.
- Prescribe medication or dosage.
- Promise final prices before examination.
- Guarantee treatment results.
- Confirm appointments without clinic approval.

The assistant must hand off immediately when the patient mentions severe pain, swelling, bleeding, fever, trauma, pregnancy, medication, allergies, or a complaint.
