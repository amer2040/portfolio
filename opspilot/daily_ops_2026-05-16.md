# OpsPilot AI Daily Ops - 2026-05-16

## Fastest Revenue Opportunity

Offer: 72-Hour AI Lead Rescue Sprint for clinics, beauty centers, and course centers.

Promise: turn scattered WhatsApp/Facebook/Instagram inquiries into a simple lead sheet, priority labels, follow-up reminders, and a daily owner report within 72 hours.

Founder price for first clients:
- Setup: $149-$299 one-time
- Optional care plan: $79-$149/month

Why now:
- Small businesses already lose leads because replies are slow and owners cannot see daily numbers.
- Delivery can be done with Google Sheets + Forms + Make/Zapier/n8n + email/Telegram first, without paid WhatsApp API.
- It is easier to sell as a fast operational cleanup than a vague AI chatbot.

## Delivery Playbook MVP

1. Discovery: channels, services, prices allowed, working hours, lead statuses, owner report preference.
2. Build lead table: name, phone, source, service, intent, score, status, next action, notes.
3. Add intake form or manual entry link for staff.
4. Configure daily report to email/Telegram first.
5. Add hot lead rule: phone + service + booking intent = same-day follow-up.
6. Test with 20 sample leads.
7. Run 7-day tuning and convert to monthly care plan.
